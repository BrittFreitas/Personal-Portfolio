RECOVERY ROAD

The Deets

Recovery Road is a substance use recovery app. It's purpose is to act as a tool for folks who are seeking to reduce or stop their use to develop awareness of their triggers and track patterns in their use. Individuals can log and save details of their substance use urges in a form. It also features an optional 'sober streak' feature, which serves as tool to track the users number of days in recovery.

The Stack

  • React
  • JavaScript
  • SASS
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Firebase
